Durum

Description

Dürüm is a Turkish wrap filled typically with a doner kebab and the wrap is made from Lavash or Yufka flatbreads.

Apart from the doner Kebab, lot of other vegetables can also be added to make this a complete meal.

Ingredients

  • 0.5 cup Masoor Dal
  • 0.25 cup Chana Dal
  • 0.5 big Potato boiled
  • 0.25 cup Mint Leaves finely chopped
  • 0.25 cup Coriander Leaves finely chopped
  • 0.5 tbsp Ginger Garlic Paste
  • 0.5 tbsp Green Chilli Paste
  • 0.5 tsp Garam Masala
  • 0.5 tsp Red Chilli Powder
  • 0.5 tsp Chat Masala

Steps

  1. Wash and soak the dals overnight.
  2. Boil both the daals in separate pressure cookers.
  3. Drain excess water from the dals and let it cool.
  4. Mash the daals and roast them in a nonstick pan till dry.
  5. Add boiled potatoes and all the spices, herbs. Remove and cool.
  6. Mix well and check for spices. With greased hand make kebabs.
